Support

H2GIS team is composed of qualified professionals in GIS and informatic sciences. The team has a long experience in spatial analysis, algorithms, geospatial standards and open source technologies.

H2GIS team offers a high level support, research and development services in the following domains :

  • Spatial data modeling,
  • Spatial analysis,
  • Algorithms,
  • SDI framework.

Due to its proximity and experience in the educational field, H2GIS team proposes professional training from users to developers (theory in spatial database, practical exercises...). These trainings are driven on demand for individual or groups.

If you need any H2GIS support, please contact us at:

info at h2gis dot org
